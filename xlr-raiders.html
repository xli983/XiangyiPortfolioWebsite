XLR- Raiders

- An Indie Game made during spring semester 2024 at Georgia Institute of Technology -

Technical PrototypeDemo

About The Game

My Role: Character Artist

Tools: Photoshop, ZBrush, Maya, Blender, Unreal Engine

Genre: Multiplayer 3D TPS Game

Timeline: 2 month

Team Size: 6

XLR-Raiders is a fast pace multiplayer TPS game, where the damage output is solely based on character move speed. Developing a 20-player battle royale game using Unreal's multiplayer system and dedicated servers, where players compete until only one remains standing. Currently under development, on hold due to team members temporary being in different countries TwT.

This game was created with other five students: Prateek (Game Designer), Owen (Multiplayer Programmer), Jamahl (UI Programmer), Jason (Mechanism Programmer), Lucy (UI/UX Designer)
