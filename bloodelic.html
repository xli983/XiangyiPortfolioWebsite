Bloodelic

- An 3D Game made during spring semester 2024 at Georgia Institute of Technology -

About The Game

My Role: 3D Artist, Technical Artist

Tools: Blender, Unity

Genre: Turn-based Reverse-horror Strategy Game

Timeline: 3 month

Team Size: 15-20

This is a VGDEV 2024 Spring Game! VGDEV is a student organization at Georgia Tech where students form teams to create six games each semester. Pitched and led by Jade Spooner, I worked closely with her as the Art Sublead and Technical Artist. You can find more information about the game and play it on the official page: Bloodelic
