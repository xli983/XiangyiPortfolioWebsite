About me

Computational Media Student From Georgia Tech

Hi, I'm Jessica! I'm a rising senior at Georgia Institute of Technology, majoring in Computational Media with a concentration in Game and Media. My focus areas include game development, technical art, and digital art.

I've interned at October Media , where I worked as a Technical Artist on their upcoming 3D animation projects using Unreal Engine 5. I'm also the co-founder of Ranji Art, a company founded by Georgia Tech students to create a cross-platform drawing application that integrates generative AI assistance in a way that preserves artists' creativity.

In addition to drawing and coding, my interests include practicing Japanese kendo, playing the electric guitar, and handcrafting cosplay props and costumes.
